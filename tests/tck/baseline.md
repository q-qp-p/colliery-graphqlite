## Notes

- `skipped` scenarios are not failures — they're cases the harness's Gherkin step vocabulary or value parser doesn't yet decode. Closing the skip rate is a harness improvement task, not a parser/executor task.
- `fail` means the query parsed and ran but produced a different result than the TCK expected. Triage in [[GQLITE-T-0211]] decides bug vs. feature-request.
- `error` means the extension raised — either a clean SQLite error or, in 8 cases, a subprocess crash. Crashes are listed above and become P0 backlog items in [[GQLITE-T-0211]].
